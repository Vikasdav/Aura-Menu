The Command Center for Your Restaurant

AuraMenu unifies your entire operation, from the kitchen pass to the dining room floor, into one seamless and powerful system.

Kitchen Order Transfer (KOT)

  • Instant Sync: Orders appear on kitchen displays the moment they're entered, eliminating verbal errors and delays.
  • Order Modifications: Easily update orders with special requests or changes, with clear notifications for the kitchen staff.
  • Item Status Tracking: Chefs can update item status from 'Preparing' to 'Ready', automatically notifying servers for faster pickup.

Point of Sale (POS)

  • Visual Table Management: A dynamic floor plan shows table status (occupied, vacant, reserved) at a glance.
  • Intuitive Order Entry: Servers can quickly add items from the visual menu, reducing training time and errors.
  • Staff Performance: Track individual server performance, sales, and table turnover rates to optimize your team.

Billing & Payments

  • All Payment Methods: Seamlessly accept credit/debit cards, UPI, and Pay Later options through our integrated gateway.
  • Effortless Bill Splitting: Split bills by item, amount, or number of guests with just a few taps.
  • Integrated Tipping: Allow guests to easily add a tip for their server, boosting staff morale and earnings.

The New Guest Experience

Elevate every dining experience with innovative features designed for modern hospitality and seamless guest interaction.

NFC-Enabled Tables

Guests simply tap their phone on the NFC tag at their table to instantly pull up your dynamic visual menu. No more waiting for a physical menu, no more apps to downloadâ€”just a seamless, modern start to their dining experience.

Integrated Tipping

Our platform allows guests to add a tip directly from their digital bill. This feature makes it effortless for them to show appreciation for great service, boosting staff morale and giving your team an extra incentive to excel.

Instant Assistance Call

A dedicated "call for assistance" button on the digital menu allows guests to discreetly notify staff when they need help. The request is sent directly to the nearest staff member's device, ensuring faster, more attentive service and a better overall experience.
